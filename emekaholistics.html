Branding + E-commerce

Emekaholistics

← Back to Portfolio

Emekaholistics came to us with a clear vision — a natural wellness brand rooted in holistic living — but no visual identity to match it. The founder needed packaging that could compete on retail shelves while also converting online, and a storefront that felt as considered as the products themselves.

The Challenge

Most wellness brands fall into one of two traps: they either lean so hard into the "natural" aesthetic that they look homemade, or they go so clinical they lose the warmth that makes holistic brands resonate. Emekaholistics needed to thread that needle — premium but approachable, earthy but polished.

The product line also required a cohesive label system that could scale across multiple SKUs without losing consistency.

What We Built

Brand Identity

We developed a full visual identity from the ground up: logo, colour palette, typography system, and a set of brand guidelines the founder could hand to any printer or developer and get consistent results.

The palette leans on warm earth tones — deep terracotta, soft cream, and a grounding forest green — that communicate nature without looking like a craft fair table.

Packaging Design

Every product label was designed with both shelf presence and print accuracy in mind. We specced the files for commercial printing, including bleed, dieline alignment, and colour profiles, so what the founder saw on screen matched what came off the press.

The label system is modular — new products can be added without redesigning anything from scratch.

Shopify Storefront

The e-commerce build carried the brand identity directly into the digital experience. Product pages were structured to support the founder's storytelling — ingredients, sourcing, and the "why" behind each product — without overwhelming visitors who just want to buy.

The Result

A brand that looks like it belongs in a Whole Foods aisle and converts like it was built by a growth team. The founder launched with confidence, and the packaging has since been the single most-commented piece of the business by customers and stockists alike.
