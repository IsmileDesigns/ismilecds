Digital Marketing + Email Design

Ora Collections

← Back to Portfolio

Ora Collections is a natural skincare brand built around clean formulations and conscious beauty. The founder had beautiful products but no cohesive digital presence their email list was sitting untouched and their social content had no consistent look or voice. They needed a marketing system that actually moved product.

The Challenge

Skincare is one of the most crowded categories in digital marketing. Generic flat-lays and discount blasts don't cut through anymore. Ora Collections needed content that felt elevated and editorial the kind of marketing that makes a $40 candle feel like an obvious purchase while still being functional enough to drive opens, clicks, and conversions.

The existing audience was warm but untapped. The goal was to build email and social systems that could nurture that audience consistently without burning them out on promotions.

What We Built

Email Campaign Design

We designed a full email campaign suite using the brand's olive green and warm cream palette clean, minimal, and unmistakably on-brand. Each template was built with a clear visual hierarchy that guides the reader from the hook to the offer without ever feeling pushy.

The campaign led with product storytelling ingredients, skin benefits, and seasonal relevance before making the ask. The result was emails that got read, not just opened.

Promotional Email

We built a dedicated promotional template for Ora's seasonal "Glow Into the Season" offer, featuring their signature product jars in a rich, lifestyle-forward layout. The 20% off offer was positioned as an invitation, not a clearance push in line with the brand's premium positioning.

Instagram Content Suite

Social content was designed to match the editorial quality of the email campaigns. We created a set of Instagram templates including feed posts and story formats that showcased product photography alongside educational skincare content giving the brand a reason to show up beyond just selling.

The "Daily Glow Boost" and "Natural Skincare" content pillars gave the account a consistent voice that builds trust before the pitch.

The Result

A ready-to-deploy digital marketing system that looks and feels like a brand three times the size. Ora Collections now has email templates they can reuse every season, a social content framework that scales, and marketing assets that do the brand justice every time they hit send.
