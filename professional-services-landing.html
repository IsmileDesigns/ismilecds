Featured work

Immigration Law — Toronto

Leo Law

Brand Identity Website Client Intake Systems

The situation

Leo Law was a growing, well-regarded immigration practice operating with a web presence that communicated none of that. The site said nothing about depth of experience or focus. Referrals came in consistently — cold traffic didn’t. Intake was handled through a mix of email and phone calls that didn’t scale and quietly leaked work.

What we built

A complete brand identity: positioning, visual system, and tone of voice. A new website built around trust and conversion, not just information delivery. And an automated intake and qualification workflow that processes inquiries before anyone on the team needs to touch them.

What changed

Conversion from cold channels improved. Intake no longer requires manual triage. The brand holds across every client-facing touchpoint — proposals, digital presence, communications. The practice now looks as good as it actually is. That’s a sentence no firm should have to wait to say about itself.
