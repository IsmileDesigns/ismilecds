Our History

Built over decades. Trusted by thousands.

From a two-partner firm on Manhattan's Lower East Side to one of New York's most recognized legal practices.

1981
The Firm Is Founded

Partners Michael Phillips and David Reeves established Leolaw in a single-room office in Lower Manhattan, taking on their first family law and criminal defense cases with a commitment to client-first representation.

1996
Landmark Case Victory

Leolaw secured a $14.2M verdict in a complex corporate fraud case at the time one of the largest civil judgments in the Southern District of New York establishing the firm's reputation in high-stakes litigation.

2008
Expansion & Recognition

Following rapid growth to 80+ attorneys across four practice groups, Leolaw was named to the New York Law Journal's "Go-To Law Firms" list and opened its second office in Midtown, specializing in real estate and business law.

2019
Rated #1 in New York

With over 1,200 cases won and a 98% client success rate, Leolaw received the AV Preeminent® rating from Martindale-Hubbell and was ranked among the top 10 personal injury and criminal defense firms in New York State.
