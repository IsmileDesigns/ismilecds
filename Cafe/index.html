Our Story

Rooted in Ethiopian Coffee Heritage

Sidamo takes its name from one of Ethiopia's most celebrated coffee-growing regions the birthplace of coffee itself. We honour that legacy by sourcing beans directly from smallholder farms and presenting them in their most expressive form.

Every drink we serve is a reflection of craft, patience, and a deep respect for the land and people behind each bean. We believe great coffee is the result of an unbroken chain of care, from soil to your cup.

Our Signature Creations

Single Origin Espresso

Pure, unadulterated expression of origin. Our rotating single-origin espresso showcases the unique character of each growing region.

Signature Pour Over

A meditative brewing ritual. Our baristas carefully pour to extract the brightest, cleanest flavours from each carefully selected bean.

Ethiopian Cold Brew

Steeped 18 hours in cold water, our Ethiopian cold brew delivers smooth, complex flavours with natural sweetness and zero bitterness.
